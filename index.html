Learning how to disc golf

Everything you need to know to hit the course.

Disc golf is similar to golf in that the goal is to finish a round in the fewest amount of strokes.

Starting from the tee pad on each hole, the target is a basket in the distance that you throw discs towards. You typically start with a driver, a mid range, and finish with a putter, although you can throw any disc for any throw.

'It's an extremely cheap sport to get into, as low as a few dollars for a couple of discs, while the higher end of the sport is around $25/disc for the top of the line disc. '

    There are several different ways to throw the disc:
  • Forehand
  • Backhand
  • Sidearm
  • Tomahawk
